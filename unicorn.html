The Unicorn

A First-hand Account from Senior Researcher Crystalium Stewartium

The unicorn represents one of the most enduring and revered symbols in mythology, celebrated for its purity, grace, and magical significance. Recent research efforts have focused on analyzing artifacts attributed to unicorns, with particular emphasis on the horn fragments preserved in the archive. Preliminary tests indicate properties conducive to healing and purification, consistent with long-standing accounts in mythological literature.

The anatomy of the unicorn is much like that of a horse, only with the addition of a spiraling horn atop its head. This distinctive feature, now dubbed an alicorn by my suggestion, is the focal point of the unicorn's legendary power. Composed of a crystalline structure unique to these creatures, the horn is believed to channel potent magical energies, enabling abilities such as purification and healing. Through further research, I hope to be certain on these hypotheses.
